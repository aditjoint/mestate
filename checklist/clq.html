Data Center Colocation Land & Infrastructure Evaluation

Comprehensive Checklist for Hyperscaler/Developer Due Diligence | ADAM Consult

1. Land Title, Tenure & Legal Readiness

Ownership Type

Is the land freehold, leasehold, or government-allotted?
If leasehold, what is the lease duration and renewal terms?

Title & Clearances

Is the land title clear, dispute-free, and encumbrance-free?
Can you share title deeds, mutation certificates, and possession proof?
Are there any litigations, liens, or rights-of-way issues?

Conversion & Zoning

Has the land been converted to industrial or data center use?
Is it located within a designated Data Center Park or IT/ITES zone?
Are construction permissions and building plan approvals in place?

Regulatory Approvals

What local/state authorities have approved the land for DC usage?
Any environmental, telecom, or power NOCs already obtained?

2. Location & Geophysical Suitability

Seismic & Flood Zone

What is the seismic zone rating (as per IS 1893)?
Has a geotechnical or soil load-bearing test been performed?
Is the land located in a flood-prone, cyclone, or low-lying area?
